PERCENTAGE AND RATIO:-
Percentage - (value/total_value) X 100% = ___%

Ratio - Value/total_value

So, Percentage = Ratio X 100 

ACCURACY - (CORRECTED_PREDICTON/TOTAL_PREDICTIONS) X 100
PRECISION - (TRUE_POSITIVES/TOTAL_PREDICTIVE_POSITIVIES)
RECALL - (TRUE_POSITIVES/TOTAL_ACTUAL_POSITIVES)

🎯 Precision
Definition:Out of all the emails the model marked as spam, how many were actually spam?

Example: Suppose the model flagged 100 emails as spam. If 90 were truly spam and 10 were
important emails wrongly flagged, then:

PRECISION = 90/100 = 90%

Interpretation: High precision means the model rarely mislabels good emails as spam.

🔍 Recall
Definition: Out of all the actual spam emails in your inbox, how many did the model 
successfully catch?

Example: Suppose there were 120 spam emails in total. The model caught 90 of them but 
missed 30. Then:

Recall=90/120=75%

Interpretation: High recall means the model catches most of the spam, but if recall is
 low, many spam emails slip through.

✅ Accuracy
Definition: Out of all emails (spam + not spam), how many did the model classify correctly?

Example: Suppose you had 1,000 emails total. The model correctly classified 950 (both spam and not spam),
 and misclassified 50. Then:

Accuracy= (950/1000) X 100 =95%
Interpretation: Accuracy gives the overall correctness, but it can be misleading if the
 dataset is imbalanced (e.g., very few spam emails compared to non-spam).

👉 Think of it like this:

Precision: "When I say it’s spam, am I right?"

Recall: "Did I catch all the spam?"

Accuracy: "Overall, how often am I right?"
